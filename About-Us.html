Who are we?

AGL Construction Services is a trusted principal contractor with over a decade of experience, delivering high-quality construction services across both the public and private sectors. 
We work closely with local councils and are proud to be a reliable building partner for a wide range of clients. Specializing in everything from residential conversions to large-scale commercial renovations, we operate throughout London and the South East. 

Annually, we manage projects with values exceeding £7 million, showcasing our ability to handle complex, multifaceted work. Our core philosophy centers on safe, sustainable, and considerate construction. We pride ourselves on managing each phase of a project efficiently, while maintaining open, transparent communication with our clients.

Our goal is to build strong relationships, ensuring every project is delivered on time, within budget, and with minimal disruption.
