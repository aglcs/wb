Health and Safety at AGL Construction Services

At AGL Construction Services, the health, safety, and well-being of our employees, clients, contractors, residents, and the general public are of utmost importance. 
We pride ourselves on maintaining an exemplary track record in Health and Safety (H&S) and are committed to continuing this through stringent policies, rigorous procedures, and a proactive approach to safety on every project we undertake. 

We understand that adhering to Health and Safety regulations is the minimum requirement, but we strive to exceed industry standards. 
Our goal is to foster a culture of safety that encourages good practices and promotes continuous improvement across all levels of the business.

Beyond Compliance: Our Safety Philosophy

At AGL, we recognize that Health and Safety is everyone's responsibility. Our approach goes beyond legal compliance, aiming for best-in-class performance. 

We encourage all our team members to take ownership of safety through:
 - Regular inductions for new employees and contractors
 - Ongoing safety training and professional development
 - Frequent toolbox talks and site inspections
 - Open communication channels to report any safety concerns

By embedding this philosophy of collective responsibility across the organization, we ensure that safety is prioritized in every aspect of our work.

Training and Competency of Our Team

At AGL Construction Services, we are committed to ensuring that every member of our team is appropriately trained to meet the demands of their role while upholding the highest safety standards. 
We ensure all staff hold the relevant Construction Skills Certification Scheme (CSCS) cards, and that every employee receives comprehensive safety training, including: 

  • Site Management Safety Training Scheme (SMSTS) for all site managers and supervisors
  • Asbestos Awareness to ensure the safe identification and handling of hazardous materials
  • First Aid at Work training to provide immediate care in the event of an accident or emergency
  • Working at Height training to mitigate risks during elevated work
  • Manual Handling to prevent injuries from lifting and carrying heavy materials
  • Scaffold Inspection training for site personnel responsible for working with or around scaffolding

In addition, our team undergoes specialized job-specific training depending on their role, ensuring a highly competent workforce capable of maintaining safety standards in every task.

Proactive Safety Monitoring and Management

To ensure our Health and Safety commitments are met, we work with an external H&S consultancy that regularly monitors and audits our policies, practices, and on-site activities. 
Their involvement helps us maintain compliance with the latest legislation and industry best practices, ensuring our sites are safe, secure, and professionally managed at all times.
We also conduct frequent internal audits and risk assessments on all our sites, ensuring that we proactively identify and address any potential hazards before they impact the project or personnel.

Commitment to Continuous Improvement

AGL Construction Services is dedicated to maintaining and improving our Health and Safety standards. 
We review our H&S policies regularly, ensuring they evolve in line with changing regulations and the latest advancements in construction technology. 
By fostering a culture of learning and accountability, we aim to create safer working environments for all stakeholders involved in our projects.

Our commitment to continuous safety improvement is demonstrated by:

  • Investing in ongoing staff training to ensure the latest safety practices are followed
  • Encouraging feedback from all employees on safety measures and potential improvements
  • Incorporating new technologies and methods to enhance site safety
  • Monitoring industry trends to stay ahead of safety regulations and innovations
