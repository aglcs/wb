The Ultimate Guide to Commercial Renovations: How AGL Transformed a London Restaurant

Sep 08, 2024


Renovating a commercial space like a restaurant can be a daunting task, but with the right planning and execution, it can breathe new life into your business. 

Recently, AGL Construction Services revamped a restaurant in Soho, turning it into a sleek, modern dining destination.

The Project:
The restaurant owner wanted a fresh, contemporary look to attract a younger crowd. We worked closely with them to redesign the layout, ensuring the new space was both functional and stylish. The renovation involved installing a new kitchen, revamping the dining area, and updating the exterior for better curb appeal.

Why Commercial Renovations Matter:
A well-designed commercial renovation can significantly impact customer experience, sales, and brand image. In areas like Soho, where competition is fierce, standing out with a fresh, inviting space is crucial.

Why AGL?
With a deep understanding of commercial construction in London, we deliver tailored solutions that meet the specific needs of our clients. From restaurants to retail spaces, AGL Construction Services offers comprehensive renovation packages to boost your business.
